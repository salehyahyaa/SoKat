ClosetScan

Scan a closet with your iPhone camera. See it empty, measured to the nearest 1/16″.

  1. Tape a letter-size sheet of paper (8.5″ × 11″) flat on the closet's back wall — it's the size reference.
  2. Photograph the back wall, then the floor.
  3. Tap the corners — get the empty closet in 3D with its exact dimensions.

Runs entirely on this phone — no install, no server.

Refinement pass (optional)

Width & height captured. For maximum accuracy, take a second photo of the back wall from a slightly different spot and tap the corners again — the two measurements are averaged, cutting random error by ~30%. This is how the app reaches its 1/16″ target on large closets.
