SpaceScan

Width & height from one iPhone photo. Nothing to type, nothing to tape up.

Runs on this phone — no install, no server.

How it works

  • Outline the back wall — 4 taps
  • Height — 2 taps, confirm
  • Width — 2 taps, confirm
  • Stand up, phone at chest height
  • Whole wall in frame, floor to ceiling
  • Avoid the 0.5× lens & heavy tilt

LiDAR Scan

LiDAR is a laser depth sensor that Safari can't access — this mode runs in the SpaceScan iOS app. Aim the crosshair, tap + four times, read width & height at about ±0.5″. No photo, no taping.

Available via TestFlight — ask Saleh for an invite.

Precision — 1/16″

  1. Print the target at 100% scale
  2. Tape it flat on the surface you're measuring
  3. One photo of the target + your span, then tap 2 endpoints
  • Same surface: the target and both endpoints must lie on one flat plane
  • Closer is better — accuracy is verified live and shown with the answer
  • Check the print: the checkerboard must be exactly 8 × 5 in
