SpaceScan

Measure any space with one photo — a closet, pantry, alcove, or room corner. See it emptied, with its dimensions.

  1. Take one photo of the space or object.
  2. Tap its 6 corners.
  3. Enter one length you know (like the ceiling height) — get its dimensions, a 3D model, and an eraser to remove objects from the photo.

Runs entirely on this phone — no install, no server.
