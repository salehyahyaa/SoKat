SpaceScan

Measure any space with one photo — a closet, pantry, alcove, or room corner. See it emptied, with its dimensions.

  1. Drop a letter-size sheet of paper (8.5″ × 11″) flat on the floor of the space — no tape, it's the size reference.
  2. Take one photo showing the whole space: floor and back wall.
  3. Tap 10 points — get the space emptied, in 3D, with its dimensions.

Runs entirely on this phone — no install, no server.
