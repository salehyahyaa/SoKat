SpaceScan

Closet measurement from iPhone photos, calibrated by a letter-size sheet.

  1. Wall photo → width & height
  2. Floor photo → depth
  3. Results — dimensions + empty 3D closet

Needs: one US-Letter sheet (8.5×11″) + tape.
For rectangular closets & alcoves. Sensor: the iPhone camera (Safari has no LiDAR access).

Runs on this phone — no install, no server.

How it works

  • Sheet = the size reference
  • Tap its 4 corners to calibrate
  • Tap 2 endpoints to measure
  • Sheet flat on the measured surface
  • Stand centered · avoid 0.5× lens & heavy tilt
  • Good light · sheet and both endpoints in frame
